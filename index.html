My name is Piotr Traczyk and I am a physicist.

A high energy experimental physicist, to be exact - meaning I deal mainly with collisions of particles accelerated to very high energies in enormous accelerators. By studying these collisions, physicists are hoping to reveal the most fundamental laws of nature.

Plus, they have loads of fun and get to travel a lot.

On these pages you will find the details of my research activities, up-to-date lists of the papers I wrote and talks I gave, and some photos from conferences, schools and the like.
